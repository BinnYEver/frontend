What is Language Model Net?

Given a pretrained LM (e.g., BERT, ROBERTA), our automatic framework extracts a KG from it in an efficient and scalable way—this results in a family of new KGs (referred to as, e.g., BERTNET, ROBERTANET) that permit a new extendable set of knowledge beyond the existing hand-annotated KGs such as ConceptNet.
